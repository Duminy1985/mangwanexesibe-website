DNS guide for mangwanexesibe.co.za
----------------------------------

After registering the domain, add these records in your registrar's DNS settings:
1) CNAME record for 'www':
   - Host/Name: www
   - Type: CNAME
   - Value/Target: <your-netlify-site>.netlify.app

2) For the root domain (mangwanexesibe.co.za):
   - If your registrar supports ALIAS or ANAME, create an ALIAS/ANAME pointing to <your-netlify-site>.netlify.app
   - Otherwise use the A records provided by Netlify (they will show in the Netlify dashboard).

Then in Netlify: Site > Domain management > Add custom domain > mangwanexesibe.co.za and follow instructions.
